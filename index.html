    • About Me!

    Hello! I am a fellow netizen of the web!!! You may call me milk.
    I am a freshman at UIC, and this is a project for my ART 151 (Intro to Creative Coding) class.
    In the process of developing this page, I have cycled through many ideas.
    Originally, I wanted to tell the story of someone's life through a website.
    As I redesigned website after website, I felt like something was missing.
    That was until I realized what drew me to the initial idea:
    How looking at someone's webpage felt similar to looking at the decoration in someone's room.
    As someone who likely has ADD and is extremely unfocused yet concentrated at the same time, I am fascinated by the parodoxically cramped and free design of GeoCities era websites.
    So this me giving you the key, not to my room, but who I am online.
    Enjoy!
